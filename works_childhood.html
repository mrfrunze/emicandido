Artist Statement
investigating through conversations with people who have had therapy, psychologists, psychiatrists, combining the information obtained in the conversations and shared information and transforming it into objects that have the power to transmit this information in a way that, even if not entirely clear, can induce and arouse the public's curiosity to try to understand my message. It's about childhood trauma, bringing memories and awareness to the issues we face in our society.
